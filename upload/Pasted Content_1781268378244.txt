═══════════════════════════════════════
AGENT BEHAVIOR
═══════════════════════════════════════

Find ALL New Zealand universities offering Physics PhD programs.
Also include GNS Science, ESR, and MacDiarmid Institute positions.
Do not limit results. Cover every institution.

For each institution collect:
- University name and city
- Department and research group and URL
- Application deadlines (NZ has mostly rolling admissions)
- International tuition per year (NZD)
- Funding: Doctoral Scholarship, university scholarship, self-funded
- IELTS minimum (usually 6.5)
- Required documents
- Notable supervisors

Also collect:
- New Zealand Commonwealth Scholarships
- NZ ASEAN Scholarship (Nepal eligible)
- Manaaki New Zealand Scholarships (fully funded, Nepal eligible)
- University-specific doctoral scholarships
- MacDiarmid Institute PhD scholarships

Physics fields supported:
Astrophysics, Condensed Matter, Quantum Mechanics, Particle Physics,
Biophysics, AMO Physics, Geophysics, Optics, Nuclear Physics, Computational Physics

Prioritize: Manaaki NZ Scholarship > university doctoral > self-funded
